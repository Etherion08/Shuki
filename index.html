Anorectal & Cyst Treatments for Women

Expert Ayurvedic care for piles, fissures, fistulas, and cysts — gentle, effective, and completely natural treatments tailored for women's physiology.

Conditions We Treat

Dr. Pallavi specializes in treating a range of anorectal conditions affecting women. Each condition requires a unique Ayurvedic approach for effective, lasting relief.

  • Piles (Hemorrhoids)

    Swollen veins in rectum causing pain, bleeding, and discomfort

  • Anal Fissure

    Painful tear in anal lining causing severe pain during bowel movements

  • Fistula-in-Ano

    Abnormal tunnel between anal canal and skin causing drainage and infection

  • Cysts

    Fluid-filled sacs requiring gentle Ayurvedic management and removal

Internal Herbal Medicines

Dr. Pallavi prescribes customized herbal formulations based on your specific condition (Piles, Fissure, Fistula, or Cysts) and body constitution. These work from within to improve digestion, reduce inflammation, promote healing, and prevent recurrence.

Arshoghni Vati

Classical Ayurvedic tablets that reduce pile mass, control bleeding, and relieve pain.

Triphala Churna

Natural bowel regulator that prevents constipation — the root cause of piles and fissures.

Abhayarishta

Fermented herbal tonic that improves digestion and ensures smooth elimination.

Custom Kashayam

Personalized decoctions prepared by Dr. Pallavi based on your Prakriti and condition.

Jatyadi Ghrita

Medicated ghee for fissure healing and tissue regeneration.

Gandhaka Rasayana

Purified sulfur preparation effective for fistula and cyst management.

External Ayurvedic Therapies

Localized treatments that provide immediate relief from pain, itching, and swelling. These applications are prepared fresh at our clinic using organic herbs.

Sitz Bath with Triphala

Warm herbal sitz baths that reduce swelling, cleanse the area, and promote healing. Recommended daily during treatment for piles and fissures.

Herbal Ointment (Lepam)

Anti-inflammatory herbal paste with neem, turmeric, and yashtimadhu applied externally to reduce pile mass, heal fissures, and soothe irritation.

Agni Karma (Thermal Cauterization)

Controlled thermal therapy using specialized Ayurvedic instruments to cauterize and shrink pile masses effectively. Performed under local anesthesia by Dr. Pallavi with minimal discomfort.

Kshara Karma (Caustic Therapy)

Application of medicated alkaline paste (Kshara) to pile masses causing controlled chemical cauterization. Highly effective for internal and external hemorrhoids with minimal recurrence.

Para-Surgical Procedures

Dr. Pallavi is specially trained in Ayurvedic para-surgical techniques that offer effective alternatives to conventional surgery. These procedures are minimally invasive, performed on an outpatient basis, and ensure faster recovery with minimal complications.

Kshara Sutra Therapy

A minimally invasive Ayurvedic para-surgical procedure using medicated thread for fistula-in-ano and advanced piles. The thread gradually cuts and heals the tract simultaneously, preventing recurrence.

Cyst Removal

Gentle Ayurvedic surgical removal of cysts with herbal wound care. Post-operative healing is accelerated with medicated dressings and internal medicines.

Agni Karma for Piles

Thermal cauterization using heated Ayurvedic instruments to destroy pile tissue. Ideal for bleeding piles and small to medium hemorrhoids.

Kshara Application

Direct application of medicated alkaline compounds to pile masses causing controlled tissue destruction and subsequent healing. Effective for non-bleeding piles.

Why Choose Para-Surgical?

These procedures avoid the complications of modern surgery such as anal stenosis, incontinence, and recurrence. They are cost-effective, require no hospitalization, and allow you to resume normal activities within 24-48 hours.

Diet & Lifestyle Guidance

Diet is 70% of the cure. Dr. Pallavi provides detailed dietary charts and lifestyle modifications that prevent recurrence and promote long-term healing.

Foods to Include

  • Warm water throughout the day
  • Ghee in moderate quantities
  • Papaya and figs daily
  • Buttermilk with roasted cumin
  • Leafy greens and vegetables
  • Soaked raisins and black dates

Foods to Avoid

  • Spicy and fried foods
  • Processed and fast foods
  • Excessive tea and coffee
  • Maida (refined flour) products
  • Alcohol and smoking
  • Sitting for long periods

Daily Routine Tips

Wake before sunrise, drink warm water upon waking, practice gentle yoga (avoid heavy squats), take 10-minute walks after meals, and never suppress the urge to defecate.
